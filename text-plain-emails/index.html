#perspective

Why is it, that a lot of emails that are sent to me (more than often against my
will) happen to be text/html only with no fallback to text/plain?

Here it is, once and for all:

Using either text/plain or text/html only is NOT the solution to better usabil
ity, use BOTH and let the client decides!

Let's not even talk about the people who uses text/plain as a mean to promote
text/html. What a shame.
