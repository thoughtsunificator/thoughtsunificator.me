Why is it, that a lot of emails that are sent to me (more than often against my will) happen to be text/html only with no fallback to text/plain?

Here it is, once and for all:

**Using either text/plain or text/html only is NOT the solution to better usability, <u>use BOTH and let the client decides!</u>**
